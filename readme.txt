== Description ==

<a href="https://circlefund.org" target="_blank"><strong>Circle Fund</strong></a> includes a powerful donation page builder which you can embed in WordPress.  It helps your nonprofit or member organisation quickly add highly optimized donation forms, crowdfunding campaigns and event ticket pages to your website in minutes. 

Circle Fund provides simple hosted donor management and fundraising software. With no subscription fees, it is free to join. You get donor, prospect and grant management features you need for your nonprofit or member organization. You have access to social collaboration features such as a newsfeed, people directory, file management, event calendar and a task manager. Integrations include Google Drive, Google Calendar and MailChimp for e-mailing your donors.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the zipped file and upload to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. After signing up on CircleFund.org, create a donation page from the Dashboard in the E-commerce or Events submenus and copy the Donate URL.
4. Use the shortcode [donate url="https://www.gocirclefund.com/campaign" width="800" height="1500"] to embed your donation form or crowdfunding page. 
5. Adjust the width and height parameters in the shortcode to suit your page.  You may want to change your WordPress page template to Full Width (see the right side column or your theme options) to make your page large enough to view the donate page and hide the scrollbars.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is the shortcode I need to use? =

[donate url="https://www.gocirclefund.com/campaign" width="800" height="1500"] which can be added to any page or post. You an edit the width and height parameters as you wish.

= Where do I get the campaign URL from? =

Just sign up for a free account at circlefund.org, and login to the Dashboard /Fundraising / E-commerce or Dashboard / Fundraising / Events pages to use our online page builder to customize and generate your URL and connect to Stripe.com.  All payments you collect will go directly into your Stripe account which you can transfer at any time to your bank account.

= Do you do custom WordPress Development for nonprofits? =

Yes, just visit https://www.circlefund.org/wordpress/ for more information.
